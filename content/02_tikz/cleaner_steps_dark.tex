        \item Add remaining neighbors that are above the \code{white!70!black}{boundary threshold} (\(Q_b = \SI{2}{\pe}\))
        \item Remove pixels that have less than \(N\) neighbors arriving within a given timeframe (\(t = \SI{5}{\nano\second}\))
